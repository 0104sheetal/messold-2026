Who We Are

Reinventing the advertising & marketing landscape.

Flashback to the vibrant year of 2016 when a dynamic collective of passionate IIT alumni joined forces to birth Messold Technologies. Their mission? Nothing short of a revolution in the advertising and marketing realm.

These visionaries envisioned a world where targeted advertising wasn't just a numbers game but a performance-driven symphony, with Messold as the maestro conducting the show.

At the heart of Messold's inception was a commitment to reinventing the advertising landscape. The founders, armed with their IIT prowess and a mission to redefine digital relationships, ingeniously introduced a performance-based strategy that set ablaze the traditional norms of the industry.

This strategy became the secret sauce empowering e-commerce businesses to not just reach but resonate with their ideal audience in ways previously unimagined.

But it wasn't just about algorithms and metrics for these pioneers. No, Messold's founders had a bigger dream - to inject warmth and authenticity into a digital world that often felt cold and impersonal. They envisioned a future where every interaction with a brand wasn't just a transaction but a heartfelt connection that touched the very essence of the customers' hearts.

So, armed with their IIT prowess and a mission to redefine digital relationships, Messold set forth on an epic journey. Their destination? To foster meaningful and lasting connections in the ever-evolving landscape of the digital age.

It's not just a company; it's a movement, a testament to the belief that in this tech-dominated world, a touch of humanity can make all the difference. Join us on this journey, where innovation meets heart, and digital relationships transcend the ordinary. 🚀💙
