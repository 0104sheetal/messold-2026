Founded by designer and entrepreneur Yuti Edwards, Udd is an Indian ethnic wear brand that breathes life into the vibrant tapestry of India's folk art. Inspired by the kaleidoscope of rural India's traditions, Udd creates designs that celebrate Kalamkari, Rabari, Mughal, and other heritage art forms, bringing them into the wardrobes of modern, free-spirited women around the world. Udd is more than a fashion label — it's a platform for artisans. Rural, tribal, and urban craftspeople translate centuries-old techniques into wearable art, making each garment a piece of India's cultural soul. The brand embodies slow fashion, sustainability, and a deep respect for craftsmanship.

By 2021, Udd had achieved recognition in India but struggled to break into the international market. While the quality and originality of its products were undeniable, the brand's digital presence wasn't delivering the results it needed. The website suffered from high bounce rates, low conversion rates, and an audience pool that was too small to support ambitious global growth.

Optimization Initiatives

That Drive Results

Our focus was to refine the website's structure, visuals, and performance for a higher conversion rate. Every optimization was made to improve the user journey — from discovery to checkout — resulting in improved engagement and revenue.

We introduced key CRO-driven enhancements aimed at improving user retention, checkout completion, and sales. These initiatives were backed by analytics and real-time user insights for consistent performance uplift.

1

Shoppable Videos

Shoppable videos transform storytelling into a seamless shopping experience. Customers can interact with products directly from the video — exploring details, prices, and add-to-cart options without leaving the frame. This creates an emotional connection and encourages impulse buying while enhancing session duration and overall engagement.

Smart Product Categorization

Well-defined product categorization ensures effortless discovery and improves the overall browsing experience. Sorting products by collection, category, fabric, color, or occasion helps customers find what they need faster. This structured navigation reduces decision fatigue, keeps users engaged longer, and ultimately increases the chances of checkout completion.

Multilingual & Multi-Currency Support

Catering to global audiences requires localized experiences. Integrating multilingual support and currency conversion allows customers to shop in their preferred language and currency — removing friction and confusion during checkout. This feature not only improves inclusivity but also boosts international sales and user satisfaction.
