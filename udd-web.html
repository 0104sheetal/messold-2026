UDD Studio crafts high-end conceptual garments. Their online destination needed to feel less like an eCommerce listing, and more like a permanent digital retrospective. We worked directly with their creative directors to discard standard catalog layout parameters entirely, moving instead toward a spacious structural layout that prioritizes high-resolution editorial format assets.

The Conflict

Identifying the barriers holding back a premier global fashion house.

Fragile Mobile Scaling

01

High-end fashion decisions happen on mobile viewports. The legacy framework forced high-resolution visuals into compressed grids, destroying detail visibility.

Narrative Fragmentation

02

The collection storytelling narrative stopped completely at the Product Detail Page (PDP), dropping consumers into cold transactional tables rather than rich storytelling.

Core Web Vitals Decay

03

Unoptimized application scripts, unmanaged asset handling, and layout shifts resulted in an aggregate mobile performance score of 32.

Cart Checkout Abandonment

04

A multi-step legacy checkout path generated significant drop-offs during high-intent conversion moments.
